Profile Summary

Dr. Deepak M D has completed his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from VTU, Belagavi. He is an Assistant Professor at GITAM School of Technology, Bengaluru, with over 15 years of teaching experience. His research areas include Medical Image Processing, Image Analysis, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. He is a lifelong member of ISTE.
